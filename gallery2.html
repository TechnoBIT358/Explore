DELHI



Top 5 places in Delhi.


1. Lal Qila - Travel back to history with this amazing piece of architecture. There is a museum inside the fort and also shops to buy souvenirs.
2. Street Food at Chandni Chowk including Karim's Lane - From jalebis made in desi ghee to chaat to parathas and the best part if you are a non vegetarian - Karim's with its mouth watering kebabs, qormas and what not. this place is the best place to eat in India - without competition.
3. Humayun's tomb with Nizamuddin street food - Humayun's tomb is the most beautiful architectural building in Delhi with a rich history dating back to centuries. Adjacent to Humayun's tomb is the famous food street at Nizamuddin. Not to miss.
4. Qutub Minar - Just because its Qutub minar
5. Shopping at Sarojini Nagar - Sarojini nagar market along with babu market must be the largest garment shopping hub in the country. In here, you will find things at prices quarter of those at malls and other places. Half of Delhi shops here, although many dont admit :) Even without any shopping, this place is worth visiting once.
